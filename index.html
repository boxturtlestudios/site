Welcome to Box Turtle Studios

About Us

Box Turtle Studios is a small game development company with big ambitions. We have many games in the making, ranging from RPGs to competitive multiplayer games. Box Turtle Studios is run by a small group of middle schoolers who all hope to make fun games for everyone to play. The company was founded in 2018 when we thought, "Hey, let's make a game!". Since then we have given up on, scrapped, and sadly lost game files until we released our first game, Battle Blox.

Featured game

Battle Blox

Battle Blox is a 1v1 platformer game using the same PC and same keyboard. The goal of the game is to knock your opponent into the void.
