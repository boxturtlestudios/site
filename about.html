About Us

Box Turtle Studios is a small game development company with big ambitions. We have many games in the making ranging from RPGs to competitive multiplayer games. Box Turtle Studios is run by a small group of middle schoolers who all hope to make fun games for everyone to play. The company was founded in 2018 when we thought "Hey, let's make a game!". Since then we have gave up on, scrapped, and sadly lost game files :( until we realeased our first game Battle Blox.
